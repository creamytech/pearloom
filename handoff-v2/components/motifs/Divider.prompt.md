Ornamental section dividers — a centered ornament flanked by fading hairlines. The dressier, more occasion-specific cousin of the woven `Thread`; use between site sections, above a date, under a chapter title.

```jsx
<Divider ornament="fleuron" />
<Divider ornament="cross" ink="var(--lavender-ink)" />   {/* memorial */}
<Divider ornament="infinity" accent="var(--pl-terra)" /> {/* anniversary */}
```

Ornaments: `fleuron pearl diamond sprig infinity sun cross`. Reach for `Thread` for the everyday brand divider; reach for `Divider` when a section wants a little more ceremony.
