The section kicker — a mono-uppercase label with a 1px gold rule, the quiet line that opens a section above a Fraunces heading (BRAND §4).

```jsx
<Eyebrow>The three acts</Eyebrow>
<h2 className="pl-display">Woven in an afternoon</h2>
```

`rule` places the gold hairline: `leading` (default), `trailing`, `both`, or `none`. Keep the text short — two or three words.
