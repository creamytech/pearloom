# AI workflow audit ‚Äî wizard, generation, editor ‚Äî 2026-06-09

## 0 ¬∑ Headline

**The words are brilliant; the visuals don't listen to the user's
photos, and the AI moments lack consistent polish.**

The story pipeline is genuinely strong: per-occasion voice directives
(solemn/playful/ceremonial/‚Ä¶) are threaded through every Claude pass,
fact-sheets ground the chapters, critique passes remove ungrounded
claims. A memorial *reads* like a memorial. But:

1. **Uploaded photos never influence the palette.** The single
   biggest "beautiful out of the box" gap. Smart palettes consider
   occasion/venue/vibe ‚Äî not the actual photos. Palette-from-photo
   exists but only as a manual editor step. *(Being fixed: a "From
   your photos" palette tile in the wizard.)*
2. **Chapter layouts are randomly cycled**, not mood- or
   occasion-aware. 48 layout variants exist; generation uses a
   dumb anti-repeat cycle. *(Structural ‚Äî see backlog.)*
3. **`eventDetails` never reach the prompts.** A memorial's
   "in memory of Eleanor" never makes it into the chapters; a
   graduation never names the school. *(Being fixed: threaded into
   the corePass fact-sheet with hard rules.)*
4. **The 70-pack catalog is invisible to generation** ‚Äî packs are an
   editor/store artifact; generated sites use Editions (5) +
   occasion defaults only. *(Deliberate per the Edition design, but
   worth a `recommendPack()` follow-up.)*

## 1 ¬∑ What's already good (don't break it)

- Voice ‚Üí every pass (corePass, critique, poetry) with per-voice
  guidance/pronouns/banned-words tables.
- Occasion-aware defaults for kit, motifs, divider, fonts, density,
  texture (`lookDefaultsFor`, `pickMotifs`, `pickFontPair`,
  `recommendTextureFor`) ‚Äî all verified live.
- Edition recommendation at render time (memorial ‚Üí Quiet,
  bachelor ‚Üí Postcard Box‚Ä¶).
- Manifest pre-warm (same wizard signature ‚Üí editor in ~600ms) and
  background decor cooking.
- Graceful degradation on most passes (vision, critique, DNA, poetry
  are non-fatal); `inline-rewrite` returns the original text on
  failure; `pear-critique` falls back to rule-based suggestions.
- GuestPearChat (published-site concierge): streaming, passport-aware,
  the best AI moment in the product.

(bulk, 3‚Äì5 days); SSE streaming of intermediate pipeline passes into
